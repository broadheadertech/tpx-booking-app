## Key Insights from Session

### Customer Perspective (Juan)
- Points must be **immediately understandable** (peso value display)
- **Physical perks** (VIP lounge) matter as much as digital rewards
- **Flexibility** in redemption (services + products) is essential
- **Streaks** create emotional investment without pressure

### Barber Perspective (Marco)
- Keep staff compensation **simple** (commission-based)
- **Never penalize** barbers for customer loyalty redemptions
- Barbers want to **know VIP customers** before they sit down
- Skip unnecessary features (tipping) - focus on what matters

### Branch Admin Perspective (Diane)
- Wallet top-ups **fund the loyalty program** (self-sustaining)
- Need **local control** over promos and pricing
- Track **streaks as loyalty health** indicator
- Physical VIP perks **measurably improve retention**

### Strategic Scope Decisions
- **Removed referral system** - abuse risk, complexity
- **Removed daily bonuses** - unnatural behavior
- **Removed friend activity** - privacy concerns
- **Removed in-app tipping** - not needed now
